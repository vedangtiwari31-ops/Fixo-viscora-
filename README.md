# 🚀 The Vision

Across cities, villages, pipelines, irrigation canals, and water networks, billions of liters of water flow every day. Most of that kinetic energy is lost forever.

**VISCORA changes that.**

VISCORA is an intelligent autonomous energy platform that converts water flow into electricity while using AI-powered agents to monitor, optimize, predict, and manage the entire system with minimal human intervention.

Instead of merely generating power,

## 🌟 VISCORA Creates a Self-Thinking Energy Ecosystem.

---

# 🌍 Problem Statement

Large amounts of renewable kinetic energy present in flowing water remain untapped.

Current systems face:

- Energy wastage from water distribution networks
- Lack of intelligent decentralized power generation
- Limited real-time monitoring
- Absence of autonomous optimization systems

---

# 💡 Our Solution

VISCORA combines:

- 🌊 Micro Hydropower Generation
- 📡 IoT-Based Monitoring
- 🧠 Agentic AI Intelligence
- ⚙️ Autonomous Decision Making
- 📊 Real-Time Analytics

to create a smart, self-optimizing renewable energy ecosystem.

---

# 🧠 Agentic Intelligence Layer

Unlike traditional monitoring systems, VISCORA doesn't just collect data—it acts.

### 🔍 Monitoring Agent
Tracks water flow, turbine performance, voltage, current, and battery health.

### 📈 Prediction Agent
Forecasts energy production and system behavior.

### ⚙️ Optimization Agent
Maximizes power generation and storage efficiency.

### 🚨 Maintenance Agent
Detects anomalies and predicts failures before they occur.

### 🤖 Decision Agent
Autonomously adjusts system operations based on real-time conditions.
